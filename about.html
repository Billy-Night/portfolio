A brief intro

I’m Billy Nightingale, a web designer and developer based in Capbreton, which is part of the Landes region in France. I help small local businesses create modern, high-performing websites that are fast, search-engine friendly, and designed to support real business growth.

With a background in mechanical engineering and a strong focus on problem solving, I combine technical development with thoughtful design to build websites that are both functional and visually engaging. My work ranges from WordPress websites for local businesses to custom web applications and digital tools designed to simplify operations and improve online presence.
