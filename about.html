THE PROCESS

Depending on the project I have a four phase approach, discovery, design and develop

PHASE 1

Discovery

In the discovery phase I have a discussion with my client and study their requirements and issues, I create mood boards and we discuss design options and available solutions to their issues. This includes research into competitors and design direction.

PHASE 2

Design

In the design phase, I present a style guide with a selection of fonts pairs and a colour palette with low-fidelity wireframes, once approved we move to high-fidelity wireframes.

PHASE 3

Development

In the development phase, I begin developing the project and optimising it for production, this includes building out a fully customised system using reusable components and optimising images and fonts for web performance, creating a custom layout system with equal and fluid spacing. All of my designs are crafted to be fully responsive and fluid, they're optimised for all major devices.

PHASE 4

Deployement

The deployment phase is the stage I connect the domain and insure the site is secure with SSL, then launch the site and begin to optimise the SEO and insure stability via test using various tools like Google Search Console and Ahrefs auditing, I run Lighthouse performance tests to ensure page speed and accessibility are optimum.
